Getting Started

Welcome to our app! Here's how to get started:

  1. Download and install the app from the App Store
  2. Explore the main features and customize your preferences
  3. Enjoy seamless audio content and prayers

Key Features

  • Lectures: Stream high-quality religious audio
  • Prayers: Access curated prayers and supplications
  • Favorites: Save content for offline listening

Troubleshooting

  • App won't start: Restart your device and ensure the latest version is installed
  • Performance issues: Close other apps and free up storage
  • Streaming issues: Ensure you're connected to the internet

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the app offline?

Some features are available offline, but you'll need an internet connection to sync and access all content.

Is my data secure?
