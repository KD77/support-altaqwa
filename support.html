Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, simply download the app and tap "Sign Up" on the welcome screen. Enter your email address, create a password, and follow the verification steps.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. All your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We follow industry best practices and comply with relevant data protection regulations.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen. Enter your email address and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.

Can I use the app offline?

Some features are available offline, but you'll need an internet connection for full functionality and to sync your data across devices.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.
